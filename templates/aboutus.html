Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a powerful and reliable tool to help individuals and businesses detect any tampering or alteration of digital images. Our goal is to help protect the integrity and authenticity of images and prevent their misuse in today's increasingly digital world. We strive to provide our users with accurate and efficient detection results, using the latest technology and techniques to identify any signs of tampering. We are committed to delivering an intuitive and user-friendly platform that can be used by anyone, regardless of their technical expertise. We wish to empower our users to trust the images they see, and to make informed decisions based on accurate and reliable information.


Our Values

  • Accuracy: We value accuracy as the foundation of our webapp, striving to provide reliable and precise detection results to our users.
  • Transparency: We believe in being transparent with our users about our detection methods, the limitations of our technology, and the reasons behind our findings.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to providing an easy-to-use platform that can be accessed by anyone, regardless of their technical expertise.
